Honestly is the first production-ready identity infrastructure for AI agents
using zero-knowledge cryptography. As autonomous AI agents proliferate across
crypto trading, code generation, and API orchestration, there's no way to
verify if an agent is who it claims to be or if it's trustworthy. We solved

reputation thresholds without revealing actual scores, and prove capabilities
without exposing proprietary architectures. We prevent replay attacks with
Redis-backed nullifier tracking and provide cryptographic signatures for
authentication. This isn't a whitepaper - we have 13,000 lines of production
code, real ZK circuits, and are ready to integrate with LangChain, AutoGPT,
and every major AI framework. We're building the missing trust layer for the
AI agent economy, exactly when regulation (EU AI Act, NIST frameworks) and
billion-dollar fraud risks make it critical. Think of it as HTTPS for AI
agents - the infrastructure that makes autonomous systems trustworthy by
default.
